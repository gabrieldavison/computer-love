deepmind photography

The following images were generated using Google's deep dream computer vision program. This is a neural network that finds and enhances patterns within images. The resulting images are heavily distorted. Lines and colour are morphed into psychedilc swirls and curves are morphed into animals and faces. Sometimes the image is completely unrecognizable from the original file. This gallery of images was created by feeding some of my own photographs into deep dream generator. Modifying the settings can give all sorts of different results even when the same source image is used each time.

Use data-src for image source to prevent all images being loaded immediately by browser
